Pancakes Recipe

Description

A simple recipe for classic thin pancakes to try at home!

Ingredients

Makes approximately 3 servings.

  • 300g Flour
  • 100ml Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon of oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of sugar (you can add more depending on your preference)
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients together into one mixing bowl. I prefer to combine the dry ingredients first and then the wet ingredients.
  2. Heat up and add oil to a frying pan until it feels warm when you hover your hand above it.
  3. Pour some of the mixture into the frying pan and tilt the pan so the mixture evenly covers the surface of the pan.
  4. Flip the pancake once you see the edges start to go brown.
  5. Once both sides have a brown spotty pattern, transfer the pancake to your plate.
  6. Repeat until you have no more mixture left.
  7. Enjoy your pancakes with whatever toppings you like!

Helpful Tips!

  • You can use one bowl to weigh your ingredients as long as you reset the scale just before you weigh the ingredient.
  • Use a ladle or pour the mixture into a measuring jug to make pouring the mixture easier to control.
  • Don't set the heat too high, a lower heat will help you prevent burning.
  • Add a small amount of oil every couple pancakes you cook to make flipping the pancake easier.
