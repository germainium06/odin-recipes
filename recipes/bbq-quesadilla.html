BBQ Chicken Quesadilla Recipe

Description

Cheesy BBQ quesadillas that are simple and quick to make. This recipe will not have specific measurements since all the ingredients you adjust to your taste. For example, I prefer mine to be very cheesy so I end up using more cheese than most people do!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla wraps
  • Chicken breast (but any part of the chicken can work)
  • Any seasoning you like to use for chicken (I only use salt and some garlic powder)
  • BBQ sauce
  • Grated or sliced cheese

Steps

  1. Start with heating up your pan until it is fairly hot.
  2. While the pan is heating up, rub in your seasonings into your chicken and place it onto your frying pan. You can also choose to cut your chicken up before or after you cook it.
  3. Flip the chicken once you see the bottom half is no longer pink.
  4. Once your thickest piece is not pink in the middle, lower the heat and add in some of the BBQ sauce.
  5. Stir the chicken around with the sauce and then transfer it onto a separate plate.
  6. Use one side of your tortilla wrap to wipe up and absorb the leftover BBQ sauce and leave the other side on the pan to crisp up
  7. After half a minute, flip the wrap. The side facing up should be the plain side now.
  8. Transfer your chicken onto a quarter of the wrap and add more BBQ sauce if needed and your cheese
  9. Carefully fold up the wrap and let the wrap grill until it is crispy on both sides.
